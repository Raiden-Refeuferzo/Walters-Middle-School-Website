Walters Middle School

Welcome To Walters Middle School!

My main priorities are to make sure our students are safe, come to school every day to learn, and be prepared for high school. I feel when students, staff, and community work together we can achieve these priorities, as well as being flexible in California’s changing and advancing education. We have many exciting events and programs for everyone to participate. I want to encourage all parents and guardians to be involved in their child’s education. Students will be more prepared and successful when parents are involved. There are many committees for parents/guardians to participate in (PTSA, SSC, ELAC, DELAC, and Volunteering in a variety of ways). Information for all of these committees will be provided in this newsletter, during MAZE day,and the Walters’s website. I am eager to start another productive year as we move forward with full implementation of Common Core Standards and striving to achieve student readiness for college and career. Our staff is dedicated to educate and inspire. Working together as a team we will increase our ability to succeed as we increase our standards and meaningful assessments. We, as Walters’s community, are here to help students achieve their goals. I look forward to another great year at Walters Middle School.

Campus Life

Walters Middle School Campus

Walters Middle School has in total 10 building 2 of whihc are two stories, a garden, a library, and a vast space where kids take breaks cald the "Blacktop". Students during break could ussually check out book in the library or play sports on the blacktop. Classes are held from 8:15 am to 2:36 pm, during this time 10 min breaks are held when passing between periods. Lunch is held at 11 to 12.

School Clubs

Anime Club
Fans of Japanese Anime & Manga

Who: Students who are fans of Japanese anime and manga. What: We will watch an anime episode during lunch time and get to connect with other otakus for fun! When: We meet the second Wednesday of every month.

Art Club
Students who like to draw, paint, and make art.

What: We will get together once a month to eat lunch, share what we have been working on, see what other people are creating and do some art! We will also have opportunities to do some art projects around the school. When: We meet the third Wednesday of every month.

School Sports

  • Boys and Girls Basketball Teams

    Walters Middles School provides the sport basketball for both boys and girls. The teams will compete with other school such as Hopkins, Thorton, Horner, etc. The teams hold tryouts to join the team. Overall this sport in Walters is fun, challenging, and competitive.

  • Boys and Girls Volleyball Team

    Walters Middle School offers the sport Volleyball for both boys and girls. The school will compete against Hopkins, Thorton, Horner,, etc. The teams hold tryouts to enter. This sport bring a excitment to the school and brings a lot of fun.
